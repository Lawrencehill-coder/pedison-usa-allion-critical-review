Fund Overview

Allion Fund 1, LP ("the Fund") is a real estate secured investment fund designed to deliver income-oriented returns through carefully underwritten commercial real estate debt. Sponsored by Pedison USA Corporation and managed by Allion Fund 1 MGR, LLC, the Fund deploys capital into real estate-backed lending opportunities, with every position supported by tangible asset security and disciplined underwriting.

The Fund is structured as an evergreen vehicle, enabling continuous capital deployment and reinvestment aligned with long-term market opportunity across the U.S. commercial real estate lending market.

Return & Profit Participation Structure

Series A and B Units target a 9% annual preferred return, distributed from income generated by the Fund's real estate secured lending activities. Once preferred return obligations to Limited Partners are satisfied, additional net profits are shared 70% to the General Partner and 30% to the Limited Partners, aligning sponsor performance incentives with Fund outcomes.
